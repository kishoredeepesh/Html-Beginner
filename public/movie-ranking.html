The Best Movies according to Deepesh

My top 3 recommendations are :-

3 Idiots

This is an Indian classic which takes you back to your college days or if you're a college student , it will surely motivate you to live your life differently.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara is a 2011 Bollywood film about three childhood friends who embark on a life-changing road trip across Spain, rediscovering love, friendship, and the true essence of living in the moment.

Ye Jawaani Hai Deewani

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a 2013 Bollywood film that follows the journey of four friends, exploring love, dreams, and self-discovery as they navigate the excitement of youth and the realities of growing up.
